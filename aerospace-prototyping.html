Aerospace & UAV Tips

The aerospace and UAV industries are some of the biggest users of rapid prototyping technologies for both prototypes and production. What Zigitech can do for you:

  • CNC machining: precision structural components
  • Rapid injection molding: plastic parts from aerospace grade materials
  • 3D printing: weight saving and low volume production




Common Applications

Prototypes, low volume production, precision machined parts, seating and interior parts, weight saving applications

Capabilities

At Zigitech, we produce prototype and low-volume rapid parts for UAV and aerospace clients via CNC machining, 3D printing, rapid injection molding, die casting, sheet metal, vacuum casting (urethane casting), and more
